About Your Hosts

Summit & Shore

What sets Summit & Shore apart is how personal boutique management feels. We build real relationships with property owners and guests — delivering warm, professional hospitality with the operational discipline that keeps top-performing homes ahead of the market.

Whether it's providing personalized recommendations for local wineries, hiking trails, and lake activities, or promptly addressing any concerns that may arise during a guest's stay, our dedicated team goes above and beyond to exceed expectations. We look forward to meeting you!

Philosophy

Local Roots. Boutique Standards.

We believe the best short-term rental management feels personal — not corporate. Every property receives thoughtful presentation, responsive guest care, and transparent owner communication.

Our Finger Lakes team lives and works in the communities we serve. That local fluency — from seasonal demand patterns to winery weekends and lake regulations — informs every pricing decision, staging choice, and guest recommendation we make.

Owner Partnership

Guest Experience

Refined Operations

Revenue, guest experience, and logistics playbooks refined in established vacation markets — applied with boutique attention to every home.
