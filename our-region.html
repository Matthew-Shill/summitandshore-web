Our Story & Philosophy

Hospitality Managed Personally

Summit & Shore was founded to bridge the gap between institutional-grade logistics and high-end, boutique styling. We believe that a luxury vacation rental requires an on-the-ground partner who treats your property like their own asset. From seamless local municipal compliance to curated design presentation, we handle the details so you can enjoy the returns.

Where We Operate

We provide full-service co-hosting, compliance support, and editorial presentation across the key corridors of the Greater Finger Lakes area:

Keuka Lake

Cayuga Lake
