      Frequently Asked Questions

      What services does Summit & Shore provide for Finger Lakes short-term rental owners?

      Summit & Shore offers full-service short-term rental management in the Finger Lakes, including dynamic pricing, revenue management, listing optimization, guest communication, cleaning coordination, maintenance, staging, photography, and direct booking strategies. Our goal is to maximize your occupancy and nightly rates while creating a seamless, hands-off experience for property owners.

      Why choose a professional short-term rental manager in the Finger Lakes?

      The Finger Lakes is a competitive vacation rental market with seasonal demand shifts, local regulations, and high guest expectations. A professional manager like Summit & Shore ensures your property stays compliant, ranks well on booking platforms, attracts high-quality guests, and earns stronger year-round revenue through data-driven strategies.

      Does Summit & Shore help with permitting and Finger Lakes STR regulations?

      Yes. We stay up to date with evolving short-term rental regulations across Finger Lakes municipalities and counties. Our team can guide you through the permitting process and ensure your property remains compliant and operating smoothly.

      How does Summit & Shore improve occupancy and revenue?

      We use a combination of dynamic pricing tools, comp-set analysis, seasonal demand forecasting, and customized rate strategies to optimize your revenue. Beyond Airbnb and VRBO, we also drive direct bookings, run targeted SEO and marketing campaigns, and leverage multi-channel distribution to increase occupancy throughout the year.

      Does Summit & Shore manage mid-term rentals (30+ day stays)?

      Yes. We specialize in both short-term and mid-term rental strategies. Mid-term stays are ideal for corporate travelers, wine industry professionals, families relocating to the region, and guests seeking extended lakefront stays. These bookings help stabilize cash flow and reduce turnover costs during shoulder seasons.

      How involved do I need to be as a homeowner?

      Our management is designed to be fully hands-off. We handle guest messaging, turnovers, pricing, maintenance, marketing, and calendar coordination so you can enjoy passive income without the day-to-day workload. You'll receive transparent monthly reporting and real-time visibility into your property's performance.

      What makes Summit & Shore different from other STR managers?

      We focus on boutique-level service, personalized revenue strategies, and direct-booking marketing — not cookie-cutter management. Our team prioritizes property presentation, guest experience, and owner communication, ensuring your home performs as well as (or better than) the top rentals in its neighborhood. We grow your revenue while protecting your asset.
