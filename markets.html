Service Areas

We manage and host across the Finger Lakes — lakefront retreats, wine country estates, and countryside homes.

Seneca Lake

Geneva, Watkins Glen, and lakefront corridors along New York's largest Finger Lake.

Ithaca, Aurora, and waterfront homes near Taughannock and wine trail destinations.

Penn Yan, Hammondsport, and Y-shaped lake properties with vineyard views.
